Profile

"Quick-Built Constructors" is the name of our company, founded by Simeon Njolomole, a Zimbabwean entrepreneur in his mid-40s and a father of four children. Our company has been established for 15 years, offering exceptional construction services in Zambia. Our primary goal is to construct stunning homes that create everlasting memories for our clients. We are dedicated to making your dream house a reality at an affordable price.


Founder

At Quick-Built Constructors, we understand the significance of a home and the profound impact it can have on people's lives. We strive to create spaces that not only meet your functional needs but also reflect your unique style and personality. Our dedicated team of professionals is committed

to turning your vision into a reality, focusing on attention to detail, superior craftsmanship, and timely project completion. We are excited to embark on this journey with you, and we look forward to the opportunity to transform your dream house into a tangible, remarkable reality.

Throughout our years of operation, we have collaborated with numerous clients who have been highly satisfied with our outstanding results. We take great pride in our track record of delivering successful projects and exceeding expectations. In addition to constructing beautiful homes, we also provide a range of other services, including remodeling, renovations, and infrastructure development. Our team is well-versed in sustainable construction practices, innovative building techniques, and adhering to industry standards and regulations. We believe in delivering high-quality craftsmanship and ensuring client satisfaction throughout the entire construction process.
